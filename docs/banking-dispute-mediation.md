# Banking Dispute & Successful Mediation Case

**Role**: Director / Responsible Officer at a Private Company (2015â€“2023 period)

## Situation
The company suddenly received a large unexpected claim (several hundred thousand HKD) from a bank for fees related to existing loans. The reason for the charges was unclear despite multiple requests for clarification.

## Task
As the person in charge, protect the company's financial interests by seeking full explanation from the bank and escalating appropriately if needed, while maintaining professional communication.

## Action
- Repeatedly requested detailed breakdowns and justifications from the bank in writing.
- When responses remained unsatisfactory, formally lodged a complaint with the Hong Kong Monetary Authority (HKMA) as the regulatory authority overseeing banking conduct.
- Cooperated fully with the HKMA's complaint handling and mediation process.
- Provided supporting evidence (loan documents, correspondence) to substantiate the company's position.

## Result
- Through HKMA-facilitated mediation, the dispute was resolved favorably.
- The company avoided or significantly reduced the claimed fees, protecting its financial position.
- Demonstrated effective escalation, regulatory engagement, and negotiation skills under pressure.

**Skills Demonstrated**:
- Stakeholder communication (bank, regulator, internal teams).
- Dispute resolution and mediation process navigation.
- Compliance awareness and proactive risk management.
- Resilience in handling unexpected financial challenges.
