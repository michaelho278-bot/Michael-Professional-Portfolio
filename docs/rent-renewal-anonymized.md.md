# Commercial Lease Renewal & Market Adjustment Case

**Role**: Director / General Manager at a Private Company (2015â€“2023 period)

## Situation
Managed a commercial office lease that had expired. The existing rent was significantly below current market rates (approximately 40% lower based on comparable properties in the district), creating ongoing financial disadvantage for the property owner.

## Task
Renegotiate the lease to align with fair market value, protect the company's revenue, and secure a stable tenancy while minimizing disputes or legal risks.

## Action
- Conducted market research using publicly available industry reports and comparable listings to establish the current fair rental value.
- Prepared a data-supported proposal demonstrating the gap between existing rent and market rates.
- Negotiated directly with the tenant, presenting evidence and offering reasonable flexibility to reach agreement.
- Finalized and signed a new lease contract at the adjusted market rate, with clear terms on payment, duration, and default procedures.
- Documented the entire process for compliance and future reference.

## Result
- Successfully renewed the lease at a +30% rent adjustment, improving annual rental income and asset performance.
- Tenant signed the new agreement; any subsequent issues were handled through proper channels.
- Demonstrated effective negotiation, market analysis, and stakeholder management under market pressure.

**Skills Demonstrated**:
- Data-driven decision making and market research.
- Professional negotiation and contract management.
- Stakeholder communication and risk mitigation.
- Compliance with commercial leasing practices.
